# ADR-0001: VEREDA v0.1 Scope

Status: Accepted

VEREDA v0.1 compares a reference RWKV-7-like recurrent core against the
experimental Vereda Dual-State core. Both use fixed four-byte patches, the same
local decoder, data, training budget, and evaluation suite.

Lorentz geometry, dynamic patching, and ternary training are excluded from the
first comparison. They may return only as isolated ablations after the
baseline and Dual-State models are stable.
